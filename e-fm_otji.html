About E-FM Otjiwarongo

Diversifying infotainment

Equity FM is the first commercial radio station being established in Otjozondjupa region with objectives  not limited to the advancement of effective societal communication, to inspire, inform, educate,  entertain and advocate for the skills development and all matters related to social welfare.

Equity FM targets a wide variety of audiences irrespective of their race, tribe, age group and gender.  Equity FM is currently covering the Otjozondjupa region reaching 142 400 people and have definite  plans to expand to other regions soon. Equity FM broadcasts in five different local languages namely  Afrikaans, DamaraNama, German, Oshiwambo, and Otjiherero, with English being the dominant, in  order to reach a large amount of audience. Equity FM boasts of thousands of Facebook and Tweeter  followers and these numbers are increasing on a daily basis.

Institution

Azusa Campus is one of the three campuses of Living Waters Theological Seminary, a theological college of AFM in Zimbabwe, established in 1974 in Harare, Zimbabwe by Rev W Wilson. Since its inception Living Waters Theological seminary has trained many Christian leaders, ranging from pastors, teachers, evangelists; prophets, and apostles. Some who are serving in government as chaplains, counsellors and social workers. Azusa campus was established in 2010.
